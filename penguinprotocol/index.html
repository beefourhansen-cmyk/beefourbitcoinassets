Privacy & terms

Your data stays on your device.

Simple, honest, and built to respect you.

No accounts

Penguin Protocol doesn't require sign-up, login, or any personal information. All game progress — your sats, your colony, your cards — lives on your device and nowhere else.

Advertising

Ads are served by Google AdMob, which may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID) and IP address for ad delivery. That data is handled by Google, not by Bee Four Studio. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Purchases

All purchases are processed by the App Store or Google Play Store. The app never sees your payment details.

Deletion

Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local data. No data recovery is needed because no data was ever uploaded.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13.

Your rights

Under CCPA, GDPR, and similar laws you may have rights to access or delete personal data. Since the app stores nothing on external servers, exercising those rights means resetting your device's advertising identifier, or uninstalling the app.

The fine penguin print

Penguin Protocol is a simulation game. In-game satoshis are game reward points generated by gameplay. No real cryptocurrency mining takes place on your device. Rewards are not guaranteed to have monetary value. Contains ads and optional in-app purchases. No expensive hardware or electricity costs required.
