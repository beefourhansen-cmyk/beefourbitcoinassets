About BeeFour Bitcoin

BeeFour Bitcoin is a mobile Bitcoin mining rewards app and a division of Bee Four Inc. Mine Bitcoin on your phone, mint unique HoneyBee NFTs with randomized traits and rarity tiers, and choose to withdraw your earnings or donate them to verified charitable organizations — including animal rescue, reforestation, and community support initiatives.

Available on Google Play for Android and coming soon to the App Store for iOS. Free to download with optional premium bee bots available via in-app purchase.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 10, 2026

Bee Four Inc. ("we", "our", "us") operates the BeeFour Bitcoin mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide: Wallet addresses (when you request a withdrawal), charity donation preferences, and referral codes you choose to share.

Automatically collected: Device type, operating system version, app usage analytics (pages viewed, features used, session duration), crash reports, and advertising identifiers for serving relevant ads.

We do NOT collect: Your name, email address, phone number, government ID, financial account information, or any personal identity documents. You can use BeeFour Bitcoin without creating an account.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to: operate and maintain the App; process Bitcoin withdrawals to your provided wallet address; process charitable donations on your behalf; serve rewarded advertisements; improve App performance and user experience; monitor and analyze usage trends; and detect and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Advertising

BeeFour Bitcoin displays rewarded advertisements through third-party ad networks. These networks may use advertising identifiers and device information to serve relevant ads. You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. Revenue generated from advertisements funds Bitcoin rewards for users and donations to verified charitable organizations.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services including: ad networks (for serving rewarded advertisements), analytics providers (for App performance monitoring), payment processors (for processing Bitcoin withdrawals and in-app purchases), and Lightning Network service providers (for facilitating BTC transactions). Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy.

5. Charitable Donations

When you choose to donate your earnings, we process donations to verified registered charitable organizations on your behalf. We share only the donation amount with the receiving charity — no personal information is transmitted. Charitable partners include registered Canadian charities.

6. Data Security

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information. Bitcoin wallet addresses are encrypted during transmission. No personal financial information is stored on your device. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy. Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 12 months for audit and compliance purposes. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us at the email address below.

8. Children's Privacy

BeeFour Bitcoin is rated for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; request correction of inaccurate data; request deletion of your data; opt out of personalized advertising; and withdraw consent for data processing. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated policy within the App and updating the "Last updated" date above. Your continued use of the App after any changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
